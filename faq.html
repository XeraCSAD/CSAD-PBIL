If your child is above 15 years old, both you and your child will need to be present at any of our polyclinics or outpatient clinics (i.e. hospital clinics and specialty centre clinics). To do so, both the caregiver and the patient will need to go down to any SingHealth hospital/polyclinic with your original NRICs. At the clinic, please approach our Clinic Registration Counter staff to verify and authorise the access.

Verification / authorisation is not available online.

It is best to schedule appointments before your arrival to minimise delay and inconvenience.

You must bring along all relevant information on your medical condition, including X-rays, medical history/records. If you are taking any medication, please bring these along and hand them to the Nurse In Charge, who should also be informed if you suffer from any allergies.
